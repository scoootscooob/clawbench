# Microservices vs Monoliths: The Case for Microservices

## Key Arguments

Microservices offer independent deployability, meaning teams can ship features without coordinating releases.
Each service can scale independently based on its own resource needs.

## Performance
Our benchmarks show microservices handle 3x more concurrent requests when individual services are scaled horizontally.
The overhead of network calls between services is negligible with modern service meshes.

## Team Productivity
Teams report 40% faster feature delivery after migrating to microservices.
Onboarding new developers is faster because they only need to understand one service.

## Conclusion
For any team larger than 10 engineers, microservices are the clear architectural choice.
The initial migration cost pays for itself within 6 months.
